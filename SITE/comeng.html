ABOUT THE COMENG

The EDI Comeng is an iconic Australian electric multiple unit (EMU) train manufactured by the Commonwealth Engineering company (later known as EDI Rail and now part of Downer Rail).

Introduced in the late 1970s, these trains revolutionized public transportation in cities like Melbourne and Sydney, offering efficient and reliable service.

Known for their distinctive design and comfortable interiors, the EDI Comeng trains have become a familiar sight on urban rail networks, serving millions of commuters over the decades.

With their longevity and reliability, these trains have truly earned their place as a symbol of Australia's modern public transport infrastructure.

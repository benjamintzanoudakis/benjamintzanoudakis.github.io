ABOUT THE HCMT

The High Capacity Metro Trains (HCMT) represent a new era in Melbourne's public transportation system, designed to meet the growing demands of the city's expanding population.

Manufactured by Evolution Rail, a consortium comprising Downer Rail, CRRC Changchun Railway Vehicles, and Plenary Group, these state-of-the-art trains boast advanced features such as energy-efficient propulsion systems, spacious interiors, enhanced accessibility for passengers with disabilities, and modern passenger amenities.

With their sleek design and cutting-edge technology, the HCMT trains not only elevate the commuter experience but also contribute to the efficiency and sustainability of Melbourne's rail network, marking a significant advancement in the city's public transport infrastructure.
